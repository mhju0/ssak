# Meok (먹)

Cozy skilling RPG for iOS — monochrome Korean ink-wash, drawn live by a procedural stroke engine, running on the player's real weather/time/season. 100% Swift, no backend. Design spec: `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-15-meok-design.md` (decision ledger in Appendix A).
